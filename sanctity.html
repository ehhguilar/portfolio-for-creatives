  • Sanctity

    Game Art

  • This side-scrolling 2D sci-fi action adventure game is inspired by Quetzalcoatl, a leading figure in Aztec mythology. Developed as a mentorship project under the direction of Vivian Li from Skybox Labs.

    “A mysterious ancient power has corrupted the gods that help maintain order in the world, and in turn, has unleashed sickness upon your homeland. To restore order, you, the high priest, must cleanse the gods to free them and your people before all you know falls to ruin.”
