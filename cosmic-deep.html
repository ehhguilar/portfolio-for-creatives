  • Cosmic Deep

    Character Design

  • This character development mashes existing IP from H.P. Lovecrafts creature Cthulu with Japanese sci-fi mecha – giant robots piloted by humans, however, this robot has a will of its own. SNARE scuttles around to help the cult preach the word of Cthulhu, and to capture sacrificial candidates and those who wish to join the cult. It is able to transfer ones consciousness into other robotic vessels to become cultists.
