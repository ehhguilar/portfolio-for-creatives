  • Meet Nathan Aguilar and Gremmy

    Concept Artist | Character Designer | Brand Illustrator

    I’m Nathan Aguilar, a Filipino-Canadian designer turned illustrator based in Metro Vancouver. I love creating memorable characters and concepts for video games and charming illustrations for brands. In short, I make people see things — in a good way!

    If you’re in need for an odd job, I can do anything from branding and exhibit design to motion design and simple animations. I’ve always enjoyed doing a bit of anything and everything, mainly due to me wanting to learn and understand the workflow of a creative project from all ends to be a team player and support.

    If you don’t catch me working, you’ll either find me brewing new personal projects, attempting another skateboard trick for the hundredth time, playing rhythm games or diligently building another Gundam model kit. Do you have any good shelf recommendations?
