  • The Creative Creature in Question

    Nathan Aguilar is an illustrator and designer based in Metro Vancouver. As an alumnus of Langara College’s Design Formation program and a soon to be graduate of the IDEA School of Design at Capilano University, Nathan has been polishing his creative craft with a focus on illustration and concept art. With a skillset that integrates both design and illustration, he brings versatility and adaptability to any creative project. Staying active physically with freestyle skateboarding, systematic gym workouts and playing rhythm games gives Nathan abundant energy for his work. An avid gamer since childhood, he wants to use his design and illustration skills to create a career in game design.
